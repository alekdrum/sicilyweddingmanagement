FAQ

Do you work only in Sicily?

Sicily is our core expertise and where we deliver the best results. Other Italian destinations are available upon request.

What is your role vs. a wedding planner?

Our role is management: vendor coordination, logistics, timelines, and on-site delivery. We bring structure and accountability so you don’t have to manage complexity from abroad.

Do you handle legal/civil ceremony paperwork?

We guide and coordinate the process with the right local professionals. Legal procedures and official filings are handled by the relevant offices and authorized parties.

How far in advance should we book?

For April–October, we recommend reaching out 9–14 months in advance, especially for popular venues and weekends.

What does it cost?

Management fee starts from €5,000. Final fee depends on guest count and weekend scope. We confirm details during the first call.
